About Me

I have a perfectly reasonable number of fountain pens and inks — NOT!

I’m fascinated by inks that shimmer, shade, and... their whimsical glass bottles. This space is where I indulge that obsession.

I also dabble in poetry. Some of it thoughtful, or random odes to coffee...tea...insomnia. Hmm I don't think I've written an ode to ink yet. Must rectify soon! Be warned, some of my verse suspiciously dramatic and cloying when I'm in the mood.

When I’m not writing, I’m probably tinkering with mechanical keyboards, chasing that perfect creamy and thocky sound.

There’s just something really deeply satisfying about the analog. Things you can touch, you can feel and fiddle with and hopefully not break! ;)

I also love mystery novels and movies, actual life mysteries, sci-fi, fantasy... and science!

Inkantations is where I purge all my obsessions.
