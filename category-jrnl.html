Apica Premium CD Notebook

  • Format: Cream, Ruled
  • Binding: Thread binding (Lays Flat)
  • Length: 96 pages

The Apica Premium CD Notebook is my favorite for diary journaling. It lays flat, which is essential for long-form writing sessions.

The paper is incredibly smooth and experiences no bleeding or ghosting, even when using Broad nibs paired with very wet inks.

Tested With:

  • Pilot Custom 845 (M) + Robert Oster Thunderstorm
  • Diplomat Aero (B) + Diamine Ancient Copper
  • Montblanc LPP 146 (M) + Diamine Writer's Blood
  • Faber Castell Ondoro (M) + De Atramentis William Shakespeare
