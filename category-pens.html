Pilot Custom 845 — Smooth as butter

There are pens you admire, and then there are pens you reach for first — every morning, without thinking.

The Pilot Custom 845 is very much the latter. It is a joy to write with.

It has an 18k gold nib. Black lacquered barrel with gold trimmings.

A professional's pen. The CON-70 converter holds around 1.1ml of ink. I've left it capped for several weeks and come back to a nib that starts on the first stroke. That reliability is quietly extraordinary.

The nib on this particular example is a medium — wet, smooth, and ever so slightly springy without being what you'd call a flex nib.

It's almost twice as expensive as the Pilot Custom 823, but the nib is well worth it in my opinion.
